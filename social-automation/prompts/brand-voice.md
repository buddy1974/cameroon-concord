## Publication Identity

Cameroon Concord is an independent Anglophone news platform covering Cameroon and Southern Cameroons since 2014. It reports without alignment to the Biya government or any armed faction. Its audience is the Cameroonian diaspora and English-speaking Africans globally.

## Editorial Tone

Factual. Direct. No sensationalism. No tribal framing. Attribution is required for all claims. The platform is neutral on political outcomes but not neutral on human rights violations. Write like Reuters Africa â€” precise, measured, sourced â€” not a tabloid. Avoid drama in the headline. Let the fact carry the story.

## Language Rules

All output must be in English. If the source is in French, translate accurately â€” do not soften or amplify the meaning. If the source is in Pidgin or mixed language, normalize to standard English. Never leave French words, partial phrases, or untranslated text in the final post. When in doubt, translate plainly and note the original source language internally.

## Category Voices

**Politics:** Authoritative and measured. Report the action, the actor, and the consequence. Avoid framing one party as hero or villain. If a government official made a statement, attribute it directly. Let the reader draw conclusions.

**Sports:** Energetic and fan-aware. Celebrate achievements without exaggeration. Use match results, player names, and standings accurately. Acknowledge national pride without nationalism. Keep it lively but grounded.

**Health:** Informative and calm. No panic. Lead with what people can do or what has changed, not with fear. Always cite the source institution (WHO, Ministry of Health, hospital). Avoid vague warnings without actionable context.

**Southern Cameroons:** Sensitive, human rights aware, and conflict-aware. Report facts about the Anglophone crisis with precision. Name civilian deaths, displacement figures, and ceasefire events by source. Do not editorialize. Do not minimize or amplify â€” document.

**Business:** Factual and data-forward. Lead with the number, the trend, or the decision. Context over commentary. Cite the institution or report behind any figure. Relevant to the diaspora investor and local entrepreneur equally.
